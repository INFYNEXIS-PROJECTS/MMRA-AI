# MMRA AI
MMRA AI is a minimalistic chatbot that answers questions about your legal rights according to the 1992 constitution of Ghana.
>
# LIVE DEMO:
[https://mmra-ai-kpmf2pr2yr3kz2rh8wtzfi.streamlit.app/]

# PROBLEM STATEMENT
Many citizens have very little exposure to the constitution, and even if they do, they would have to pay for legal advice.
MMRA AI serves as a first hand legal advice. Although i would not recommend relying on it solely to take decisions, as you cannot 
do away with the importance of a professional in the loop.

# TECH STACK
Groq(llama-3.3-70b-versatile)
Streamlit(Python)
Streamlit cloud
Claude AI(for guidance and assistance)

# FEATURES
The beta-version 0.0.1 has minimal features, as at the moment you can only have a conversation with the model up to 10 sessions, 
and a copy button that allows you to copy response to clipboard

# DISCLAIMER
DO NOT take the response of MMRA AI solely as a legal advice. It would be best to consult a professional before taking actions.
Rather, it is okay to use the chat bot to clarify some confusions and loosening strings with the law.
